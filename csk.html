Chennai Super Kings

  1. IPL Titles: CSK has won five IPL titles, making them a successful franchise.
  2. Founding & Ownership: Founded in 2008, CSK is owned by India Cements.
  3. Home Ground: They play their home matches at the M. A. Chidambaram Stadium in Chennai.
  4. Captain & Coach: The team is currently captained by Ruturaj Gaikwad and coached by Stephen Fleming.
  5. Records: CSK has the most appearances in IPL finals (10) and playoffs (12).
  6. Other Notable Achievements: CSK has also won the Champions League Twenty20 twice.
